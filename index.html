How's My Voice Leading?

This program attempts to detect common errors in traditional four-part harmony. The following rules of voice leading are checked:

  • Vocal Range
  • Spacing Between Voices
  • Parallel Fifths
  • Parallel Octaves

Using a music notation program such as NoteFlight, export your four-part harmony to MusicXML and paste the XML content below, then press "Submit". Errors will be shown below.
